My Game of Life

This is a showcase of my newly made game of life.
I had to create one for our computer class, so i decided to create this project:

The rules of all entities are the same:
each entity can die and has a limited lifetime
every single one can get up to four children
everyone immediately starts to color the land where the entity had been
if an entity gets on the area colored by an enemy, the lifetime will decrease
